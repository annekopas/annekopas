how can i help you?

Words matter.

That's the number one thing I've learned in my experience as a professional writer and editor (plus a summa cum laude degree in English and communications/journalism). I'm game for a variety of projects:

  • feature articles
  • editing
  • content strategy
  • social media
  • web design
  • writing projects

Why me?

Because I'm genuinely invested in telling stories that matter. Because my time in a newsroom taught me to be deadline-driven. Because I'm a digital native. Because I get excited about style guides. Because content strategy requires a young, fresh take.

But don't just take my word for it.
