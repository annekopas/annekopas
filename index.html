Writer. Editor. Web designer.

Hey. I'm Anne.

I'm a storyteller at heart with a passion for the written word, both print and digital. I've been a blogger, magazine writer, literary journal editor, web designer, and more. I'm currently a full-time journalist with several southeastern Minnesota newspapers and related publications, moonlighting as a YA novelist (seeking representation). I'm a University of St. Thomas grad with degrees in Communications/Journalism and English, with a minor in pyschology. When not scouting local feature stories, you'll find me going for a run around town, baking up a storm, or reading the largest novels I can find.
